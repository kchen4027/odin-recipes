Steak

Ingredients

  • Raw Steak
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cooking Oil
  • Bay Leaf
  • Stove with Pan and Cooking Utensils

Instructions

  1. Put cooking oil and bit of butter on pan
  2. Slap steak on pan
  3. Turn on medium heat
  4. Put cubes of butter on one side of pan
  5. Swirl butter until melted
  6. Cook one side of steak and scoop butter onto the cooked area
  7. Flip steak when one side is done to liking
  8. Flip other side and accommodate butter
  9. Bay Leaf, salt and pepper, then cook for a minute
  10. When done, remove from pan and enjoy
