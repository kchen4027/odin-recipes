Steak

Ingredients

    Raw Steak
    Butter
    Salt
    Pepper
    Cooking Oil
    Bay Leaf
    Stove with Pan and Cooking Utensils

    Put cooking oil and bit of butter on pan
    Slap steak on pan
    Turn on medium heat
    Put cubes of butter on one side of pan
    Swirl butter until melted
    Cook one side of steak and scoop butter onto the cooked area
    Flip steak when one side is done to liking
    Flip other side and accommodate butter
    Bay Leaf, salt and pepper, then cook for a minute
    When done, remove from pan and enjoy
